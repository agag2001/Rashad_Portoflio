About Me

I'm a Senior Graphic Designer based in Cairo, Egypt, with over 10 years of professional experience in visual design, branding, and print media. I've been working at ICONS Integrated Solution since 2014.

My work spans across social media design, corporate branding, annual reports, brochures, mobile app UI, and large-scale campaign visuals. I specialize in both Arabic and English typography, delivering designs that resonate with diverse audiences.

I believe great design is not just about aesthetics — it's about communication. Every pixel, every color choice, and every layout decision serves a purpose in telling your brand's story.
